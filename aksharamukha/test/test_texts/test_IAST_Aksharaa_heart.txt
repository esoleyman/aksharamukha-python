aaryatriratnaanusmru'tisuutram

(kha) sam'skru'tapaat'hah'

bhaaratiiyabhaashhaayaam-aaryatriratnaanusmru'tisuutram

bhoot'abhaashhaayaam-phags-paa-dakoon-mchchhoog-gsum-jees-su-d'ran-paa-mdoo

namah' sarvabuddhaboodhisattveebhyah'

(buddhaanusmru'tih')

ityapi buddhoo bhagavaam'stathaagatooa;rhan samyaksam'buddhoo vidyaacharan'asampannah' sugatoo lookavidanuttarah' purushhadamyasaarathih' shaastaa deevamanushhyaan'aam' buddhoo bhagavaaniti.

nishhyandah' sa tathaagatah' pun'yaanaam, avipran'aashah' kushalamuulaanaam, alang'kru'tah' kshhaantyaa, aalayah' pun'yanidhaanaanaam, chitritooa;nuvyanj'janaih' kusumitoo lakshhan'aih', pratiruupoo goochareen'a, apratikuuloo darshaneena, abhiratih' shraddhaa(shuddhaa)dhimuktaanaam, anabhibhuutah' prajnj'ayaa, anavamardaniiyoo balaih', shaastaa sarvasattvaanaam, pitaa boodhisattvaanaam, raajaa aaryapudgalaanaam, saarthavaahah' nirvaan'anagarasamprasthitaanaam, aprameeyoo jnj'aaneena, achintyah' pratibhaaneena, vishuddhah' svareen'a, aasvadaniiyoo ghooshheen'a, aseechanakoo ruupeen'a, apratisamah' kaayeena, aliptah' kaamaih', anupaliptoo ruupaih' asam'sru'shht'a aaruupyaih', vipramuktah' skandheebhyah', visamprayuktoo dhaatubhih', sam'vru'ta aayatanaih', prachchhinnoo granthaih', vimuktah' paridaaghnaih', parimuktastru'shhn'ayaa, ooghaaduttiirn'ah' paripuurn'oo jnj'aaneena, pratishht'hitooa;tiitaanaagatapratyutpannaanaam' buddhaanaam' bhagavataam' jnj'aanee, apratishht'hitoo nirvaan'ee, sthitoo bhuutakoot'yaam, sthitah' sarvasattvaalookaniiyaayaam' bhuumau, sarva imee tathaagataanaam' visheeshhatah' samyag gun'aah'.

(dharmaanusmru'tih')

saddharmastu aadau kalyaan'ah', madhyee kalyaan'ah', paryavasaanee kalyaan'ah', svarthah' suvyanj'janah', keevalah', paripuurn'ah', parishuddhah', paryavadaatah', svaakhyaatah' bhagavatoo dharmah', saandru'shht'ikah', nirjvarah', aakaalikah', aupanaayikah', aihipashyikah', pratyaatmaveedaniiyoo vijnj'aih', svaakhyaatoo bhagavatoo dharmavinayah' supraveeditah' nairyaan'ikah', sam'boodhigaamii, abhinnah' sam'stuupah', sapratisharan'ah', chhinnaplootikah'.

(sam'ghaanusmru'tih')

supratipannoo bhagavata aaryasam'ghah', nyaayapratipannah', ru'jupratipannah', saamiichiipratipannah', anj'jaliikaran'iiyah', saamiichiikaran'iiyah', pun'yashriikshheetrah', mahaadakshhin'aaparishoodhakah', praahavaniiyah', aahavaniiyah'.
