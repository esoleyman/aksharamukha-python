aaryatriratnaanusmri'tisootram

(kha) sam'skri'tapaat'hah'

bhaarateeyabhaashaayaam-aaryatriratnaanusmri'tisootram

bhot'abhaashaayaam-phags-paa-dakon-mchchhog-gsum-jes-su-d'ran-paa-mdo

namah' sarvabuddhabodhisattvebhyah'

(buddhaanusmri'tih')

ityapi buddho bhagavaam'stathaagato'rhan samyaksambuddho vidyaacharanasampannah' sugato lokavidanuttarah' purushadamyasaarathih' shaastaa devamanushyaanaam' buddho bhagavaaniti.

nishyandah' sa tathaagatah' punyaanaam, avipranaashah' kushalamoolaanaam, alankri'tah' kshaantyaa, aalayah' punyanidhaanaanaam, chitrito'nuvyanjanaih' kusumito lakshanaih', pratiroopo gocharena, apratikoolo darshanena, abhiratih' shraddhaa(shuddhaa)dhimuktaanaam, anabhibhootah' prajnyayaa, anavamardaneeyo balaih', shaastaa sarvasattvaanaam, pitaa bodhisattvaanaam, raajaa aaryapudgalaanaam, saarthavaahah' nirvaananagarasamprasthitaanaam, aprameyo jnyaanena, achintyah' pratibhaanena, vishuddhah' svarena, aasvadaneeyo ghoshena, asechanako roopena, apratisamah' kaayena, aliptah' kaamaih', anupalipto roopaih' asam'sri'sht'a aaroopyaih', vipramuktah' skandhebhyah', visamprayukto dhaatubhih', sam'vri'ta aayatanaih', prachchhinno granthaih', vimuktah' paridaaghnaih', parimuktastri'shnayaa, oghaadutteernah' paripoorno jnyaanena, pratisht'hito'teetaanaagatapratyutpannaanaam' buddhaanaam' bhagavataam' jnyaane, apratisht'hito nirvaane, sthito bhootakot'yaam, sthitah' sarvasattvaalokaneeyaayaam' bhoomau, sarva ime tathaagataanaam' visheshatah' samyag gunaah'.

(dharmaanusmri'tih')

saddharmastu aadau kalyaanah', madhye kalyaanah', paryavasaane kalyaanah', svarthah' suvyanjanah', kevalah', paripoornah', parishuddhah', paryavadaatah', svaakhyaatah' bhagavato dharmah', saandri'sht'ikah', nirjvarah', aakaalikah', aupanaayikah', aihipashyikah', pratyaatmavedaneeyo vijnyaih', svaakhyaato bhagavato dharmavinayah' supraveditah' nairyaanikah', sambodhigaamee, abhinnah' sam'stoopah', sapratisharanah', chhinnaplotikah'.

(sanghaanusmri'tih')

supratipanno bhagavata aaryasanghah', nyaayapratipannah', ri'jupratipannah', saameecheepratipannah', anjaleekaraneeyah', saameecheekaraneeyah', punyashreekshetrah', mahaadakshinaaparishodhakah', praahavaneeyah', aahavaneeyah'.
