’āryatriratnānusmṛtisūtramʻ

(kha) saṃskṛtapāṭhaʺ

bhāratīyabhāṣāyāmʻ-’āryatriratnānusmṛtisūtramʻ

bhoṭabhāṣāyāmʻ-phagsʻ-pā-dakonʻ-mcchogʻ-gsumʻ-jesʻ-su-ḍranʻ-pā-mdo

namaʺ sarvabuddhabodhisattvebhyaʺ

(buddhānusmṛtiʺ)

ityapi buddho bhagavāṃstathāgato'rhanʻ samyaksaṃbuddho vidyācaraṇasampannaʺ sugato lokavidanuttaraʺ puruṣadamyasārathiʺ śāstā devamanuṣyāṇāṃ buddho bhagavāniti,

niṣyandaʺ sa tathāgataʺ puṇyānāmʻ, ’avipraṇāśaʺ kuśalamūlānāmʻ, ’alaṅkṛtaʺ kṣāntyā, ’ālayaʺ puṇyanidhānānāmʻ, citrito'nuvyañjanaiʺ kusumito lakṣaṇaiʺ, pratirūpo gocareṇa, ’apratikūlo darśanena, ’abhiratiʺ śraddhā(śuddhā)dhimuktānāmʻ, ’anabhibhūtaʺ prajñayā, ’anavamardanīyo balaiʺ, śāstā sarvasattvānāmʻ, pitā bodhisattvānāmʻ, rājā ’āryapudgalānāmʻ, sārthavāhaʺ nirvāṇanagarasamprasthitānāmʻ, ’aprameyo jñānena, ’acintyaʺ pratibhānena, viśuddhaʺ svareṇa, ’āsvadanīyo ghoṣeṇa, ’asecanako rūpeṇa, ’apratisamaʺ kāyena, ’aliptaʺ kāmaiʺ, ’anupalipto rūpaiʺ ’asaṃsṛṣṭa ’ārūpyaiʺ, vipramuktaʺ skandhebhyaʺ, visamprayukto dhātubhiʺ, saṃvṛta ’āyatanaiʺ, pracchinno granthaiʺ, vimuktaʺ paridāghnaiʺ, parimuktastṛṣṇayā, oghāduttīrṇaʺ paripūrṇo jñānena, pratiṣṭhito'tītānāgatapratyutpannānāṃ buddhānāṃ bhagavatāṃ jñāne, ’apratiṣṭhito nirvāṇe, sthito bhūtakoṭyāmʻ, sthitaʺ sarvasattvālokanīyāyāṃ bhūmoʻ, sarva ime tathāgatānāṃ viśeṣataʺ samyagʻ guṇāʺ,

(dharmānusmṛtiʺ)

saddharmastu ’ādoʻ kalyāṇaʺ, madhye kalyāṇaʺ, paryavasāne kalyāṇaʺ, svarthaʺ suvyañjanaʺ, kevalaʺ, paripūrṇaʺ, pariśuddhaʺ, paryavadātaʺ, svākhyātaʺ bhagavato dharmaʺ, sāndṛṣṭikaʺ, nirjvaraʺ, ’ākālikaʺ, oʻpanāyikaʺ, ’aihipaśyikaʺ, pratyātmavedanīyo vijñaiʺ, svākhyāto bhagavato dharmavinayaʺ supraveditaʺ nairyāṇikaʺ, saṃbodhigāmī, ’abhinnaʺ saṃstūpaʺ, sapratiśaraṇaʺ, chinnaplotikaʺ,

(saṃghānusmṛtiʺ)

supratipanno bhagavata ’āryasaṃghaʺ, nyāyapratipannaʺ, ṛjupratipannaʺ, sāmīcīpratipannaʺ, ’añjalīkaraṇīyaʺ, sāmīcīkaraṇīyaʺ, puṇyaśrīkṣetraʺ, mahādakṣiṇāpariśodhakaʺ, prāhavanīyaʺ, ’āhavanīyaʺ,

. ’āryatriratnānusmṛtisūtraṃ samāptamʻ.
