prajnyaparamitahridayasutram.

[sankshiptamatrika]

.. namah sarvajnyaya ..

.. Om namo bhagavatyai aryaprajnyaparamitayai ..

aryavalokiteshvarabodhisattvo gambhirayam prajnyaparamitayam charyam charamano vyavalokayati sma. pancha skandhah, tamshcha svabhavashunyan pashyati sma..

iha shariputra rupam shunyata, shunyataiva rupam. rupanna prithak shunyata, shunyataya na prithag rupam. yadrupam sa shunyata, ya shunyata tadrupam..

evameva vedanasanjnyasamskaravijnyanani..

iham shariputra sarvadharmah shunyatalakshana anutpanna aniruddha amala na vimala nona na paripurnah. tasmachchhariputra shunyatayam na rupam, na vedana, na sanjnya, na samskarah, na vijnyanani. na chakshuhshrotraghranajihvakayamanamsi, na rupashabdagandharasasprashtavyadharmah. na chakshurdhaturyavanna manodhatuh..

na vidya navidya na vidyakshayo navidyakshayo yavanna jaramaranam na jaramaranakshayo na duhkhasamudayanirodhamarga na jnyanam na praptitvam..

bodhisattvasya prajnyaparamitamashritya viharati chittavaranah. chittavarananastitvadatrasto viparyasatikranto nishthanirvanah. tryadhvavyavasthitah sarvabuddhah prajnyaparamitamashritya anuttaram samyaksambodhimabhisambuddhah..

tasmajjnyatavyah prajnyaparamitamahamantro mahavidyamantronuttaramantrosamasamamantrah sarvaduhkhaprashamanah satyamamithyatvat prajnyaparamitayamukto mantrah.

tadyatha- gate gate paragate parasangate bodhi svaha..

iti prajnyaparamitahridayasutram samaptam..
