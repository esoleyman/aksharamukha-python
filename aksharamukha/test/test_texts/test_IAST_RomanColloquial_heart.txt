aryatriratnanusmritisutram

(kha) samskritapathah

bharatiyabhashayam-aryatriratnanusmritisutram

bhotabhashayam-phags-pa-dakon-mchchhog-gsum-jes-su-dran-pa-mdo

namah sarvabuddhabodhisattvebhyah

(buddhanusmritih)

ityapi buddho bhagavamstathagatorhan samyaksambuddho vidyacharanasampannah sugato lokavidanuttarah purushadamyasarathih shasta devamanushyanam buddho bhagavaniti.

nishyandah sa tathagatah punyanam, avipranashah kushalamulanam, alankritah kshantya, alayah punyanidhananam, chitritonuvyanjanaih kusumito lakshanaih, pratirupo gocharena, apratikulo darshanena, abhiratih shraddha(shuddha)dhimuktanam, anabhibhutah prajnyaya, anavamardaniyo balaih, shasta sarvasattvanam, pita bodhisattvanam, raja aryapudgalanam, sarthavahah nirvananagarasamprasthitanam, aprameyo jnyanena, achintyah pratibhanena, vishuddhah svarena, asvadaniyo ghoshena, asechanako rupena, apratisamah kayena, aliptah kamaih, anupalipto rupaih asamsrishta arupyaih, vipramuktah skandhebhyah, visamprayukto dhatubhih, samvrita ayatanaih, prachchhinno granthaih, vimuktah paridaghnaih, parimuktastrishnaya, oghaduttirnah paripurno jnyanena, pratishthitotitanagatapratyutpannanam buddhanam bhagavatam jnyane, apratishthito nirvane, sthito bhutakotyam, sthitah sarvasattvalokaniyayam bhumau, sarva ime tathagatanam visheshatah samyag gunah.

(dharmanusmritih)

saddharmastu adau kalyanah, madhye kalyanah, paryavasane kalyanah, svarthah suvyanjanah, kevalah, paripurnah, parishuddhah, paryavadatah, svakhyatah bhagavato dharmah, sandrishtikah, nirjvarah, akalikah, aupanayikah, aihipashyikah, pratyatmavedaniyo vijnyaih, svakhyato bhagavato dharmavinayah supraveditah nairyanikah, sambodhigami, abhinnah samstupah, sapratisharanah, chhinnaplotikah.

(sanghanusmritih)

supratipanno bhagavata aryasanghah, nyayapratipannah, rijupratipannah, samichipratipannah, anjalikaraniyah, samichikaraniyah, punyashrikshetrah, mahadakshinaparishodhakah, prahavaniyah, ahavaniyah.

.. aryatriratnanusmritisutram samaptam..
